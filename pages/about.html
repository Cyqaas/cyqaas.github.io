Education

Arizona State University

August 2020 - May 2024

As a Computer Science student at ASU, my journey started learning Java to develop a foundation of object-oriented programming. Later learning C# and C++ to learn Data Structures and Algorithms, develop parser programs, and deepen my understanding of OOP. As for front-end development, HTML5 and CSS were used to structure and style elements which utilized API calls through JavaScript to create functional websites.

As the years passed, new skills were developed and on-going ones were strengthened. Beginning with perserverance, being consistent and having courage in the face of daunting tasks helped me develop confidence in overcoming these obstacles. Next up, communication, learning not be afraid to reach out for help and conversating with peers about topics cleared up confusion and lead to deeper understanding of those ideas through different perspectives. Finally, the importance of taking breaks as throughout various projects and assignments there were times I did problems repeatedly leading to absolutely nowhere and taking a break from these tasks helped me come back with a clear mind to identify mistakes and come up with a solution.

Overall, my time spent at ASU was well worth as I was the first in my family to obtain a college degree, obtain new, and strengthen existing skills which not only extend to my career path but personal life.
